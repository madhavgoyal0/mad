CLIMATIC CONDITION FOR VARIOUS TYPES OF CROPS

  • RICE
    1. High temperature and high humidty.
    2. Annual rainfall above 100cm.
    3. It is a karif crop.


  • WHEAT
    1. Cool temperature while growing and bright sunshine while harvesting.
    2. Annual rainfall of 50 to 70cm.
    3. It is a rabi crop.


  • MAIZE
    1. 21°C to 27°C temperature is required
    2. It is a karif crop.>


  • RUBBER
    1. Grows in tropical and sub tropical areas
    2. Temperature above 25°C
    3. Annual rainfall more than 200cm


  • COTTON
    1. High temperature is required
    2. Light rainfall
    3. 210 frost-free days
